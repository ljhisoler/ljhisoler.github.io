What is One Piece?

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda in July 1997 and has been adapted into an anime series produced by Toei Animation in 1999. Additionally, Toei has also developed fourteen animated feature films.

The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gained the properties of rubber after eating a devil fruit. With his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy explores the Grand Line in search for the world's ultimate treasure known as "One Piece" in order to become the next King of the Pirates.
