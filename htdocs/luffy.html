Description

    Monkey D. Luffy, also known as "Straw Hat Luffy,” is the main protagonist of One Piece. He is the founder and captain of the increasingly infamous and powerful Straw Hat Pirates, as well as one of its top fighters. His lifelong dream is to become the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure left behind by the late Gol D. Roger. He currently has a bounty of 1,500,000,000 Beli.


Abilities

Luffy is the captain and the strongest member of the Straw Hat Pirates.

Devil Fruit (Gomu Gomu no Mi):

  • Second Gear - Luffy increases the size of his blood vessels, pumping more oxygen and nutrients to his body. This increases his speed and power
  • Third Gear - By blowing, Luffy can inflate the bones of a particular part of his body, making it massive. These inflated body parts are capable of inflicting damage on a massive scale
  • Fourth Gear - By blowing into his arm, Luffy inflates his entire body and covers it with Busoshoku Haki. Three forms: Bounce Man, Tank Man, Snake Man

Haki:

  • Kenbunshoku (Observation) Haki
  • Busoshoku (Armament) Haki
  • Haoshoku (Conqueror's) Haki
