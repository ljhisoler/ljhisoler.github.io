Description

    “Black Leg" Sanji, born as Vinsmoke Sanji, is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates. His dream is to find the rumored chef's paradise, All Blue, which is where East Blue, West Blue, North Blue, and South Blue meet, along with their wildlife. He is one of the top four fighters of the Straw Hats, alongside Luffy, Zoro, and Jinbe. He currently has a bounty of 330,000,000 Beli.


Abilities

Sanji is among the four strongest members in the Straw Hat Pirates.

Diable Jambe - the power to heat his lower legs to a temperature so high that they glow bright red, by spinning rapidly in place, which causes his attack to be more powerful.

Haki:

  • Kenbunshoku (Observation) Haki
  • Busoshoku (Armament) Haki
